################# Prompt to Generate Data Model for VCE Study Design  ################

Can generate a the model to have following concepts 
- Study Design has Name, Summary, Subject, Board and have multiple Units
- Each Unit has Area of Study
- Area of Study has ID, Name and Description, ID being Area of Study 1,Area of Study 2,Area of Study 3 etc
- Each Areas of Study has multiple Outcomes. 
- Outcome has Outcome ID, Outcome Name, Desc and Key Knowledge. OutcomeID being Outcome 1, Outcome 2 etc
- Knowledge Topic has Name and multiple Sub-Topics
- SubTopic has Name and Details
